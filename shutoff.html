LetterC Shutoff

You can set up the LetterC to automatically shut off after a preset time. The options are self explanatory:

Play Forever - the display doesn't shut off until you do it manually using off in the color menu and pressing the submit button.

30 seconds - Shuts off after 30 seconds. Used mostly for testing.

30 minutes - Shuts off after 30 minutes.

1 hr - shuts off after an Hour.

2 hr - shuts off after 2 hours.
